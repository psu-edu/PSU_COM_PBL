Mitral Valve, Normal

An image of the atrio-ventricular plane showing the 4 valves.  Left panel, during diastole.  Right panel, during systole.  Note that each LV papillary muscle attaches to both mitral valve leaflets.  Each is centered at a commissure (AC-Anterior Commissure, PC-Posterior Commissure), and each has chordae to both cusps.  CA-Coronary Artery (which one?);  CS-Coronary Sinus;  AL-Anterior Leaflet;  PL-Posterior Leaflet;  The A(n) and P(n) number the portions of the mitral valve leaflets.  You do not need to be able to identify them.  xFT=R & L Fibrous Trigone;  HB-His Bundle.
