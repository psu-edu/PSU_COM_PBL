Mitral Valve Prolapse, Comparison

  

The image of the diseased valve is on the left.  Notice the ballooning of the mitral valve cusps compared to the normal valve on the right.  The faint blue dotted lines on the normal valve show the limits of the atrial myocardium, and within that crescent there is no myocardium, but only the thin leaflets.

Ho SY. Anatomy of the Mitral Valve. Heart 2002;88:iv5-iv10.
