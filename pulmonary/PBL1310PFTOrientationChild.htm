PBL CASE 1310:  Orientation to Spirometry, 5 year old boy
The upper left graph shows two loops of the exhaled flow (vertical axis) versus the exhaled volume (horizontal axis).  The data is derived from the volume versus time as shown on the right.  The inhaled phase (the bottom of the loops) is often not shown in the time plot.  The blue traces are before treatment, and the red traces after bronchodilator treatment in each graph.  To see how the time plot relates to the flow plot, press "NEXT".
